About

Experienced fullstack developer, database architect and pipeline engineer in bioinformatics making a pivot to quantum computing.

Specializing in scientific web applications and research workflow tools for processing, analyzing and visualizing experimental data, from large scale instruments to smaller assay measurements to downstream analysis and reporting.
